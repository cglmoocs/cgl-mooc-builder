MOOC Summary

This FREE MOOC (Massive Open Online Course) investigates the use of clouds running data analytics collaboratively for processing Big Data to solve problems in Big Data Applications and Analytics. Case studies such as Netflix recommender systems, Genomic data, and more will be discussed.

Anybody having the desire to learn about Big Data! No programming required. The graded undergraduate and graduate versions for IU students will be based and/or extended from this MOOC. Want to Know More?

Earn Badges

In this free MOOC, you will earn badges that will be permanently stored in your backpack. While there is no grade assigned in this course, you will get an opportunity to learn at your own pace by reviewing peer assignment(s) or homework(s).

Discuss & Interact

Entire course discussion will take place on the Google Plus Community Forum in different streams. You will also get a chance to interact with the instructor using Hangouts On Air while asking live questions.
